How to prepare

  1. Upload songs by naviating to Add Songs.
  2. Drag and drop files onto the upload field or select them manually.
  3. After uploading the files are parsed and the data is inserted into the database. You can changed everything like removing unnecessary parts of an title or correcting the year or even add the full information, if the MP3 file does not contain an ID3 tag. After changing something, a button appears. Click it to save the changes.
  4. After you have added all songs, you can generate the game cards by navigating to List Songs.
  5. Select all songs for which you will generate the game card by checking its checkbox at the end of the titles entry. A small symbol indicates, that the card has been printed already. By default all unprinted cards are selected.
  6. After selecting the songs, click the "Generate Game Cards" button on the bottom of the page.
  7. A dialog will open where you can see the generation state and receive the download links for the game cards. Depending on your system, the number of generated cards are reduced to {{ chunk_size }} per sheet.
  8. After downloading the game cards, you can print them on a sheet of paper. The game cards are designed to fit on a standard A4 paper. The layout fits AVERY Zweckform C32010-25 business card templates, so you can print decent cards. The cards can be directly printed two sided, if the printer suppors it. Using this feature select "Print double-sided, flip on long side".
  9. Cut the cards and start a game

How to play

  1. Shuffle the cards and make a pile with the QR codes facing up.
  2. Use your mobile phone and navigate to https://<your computer name> You will get an SSL warning caused by an self signed certificate. As the game is used locally, this warning can be ignored.
  3. Navigate to Play, select the back or front camera of your phone and scan the first card.
  4. After scanning the card, the song will be played and the game starts.
  5. After the song has been played, the player has to guess the song title, artist or release year.
  6. After the player has guessed the artist, title or year he gets points accordenly to the count of right guessed parts. Then the next card can be scanned.
  7. After all cards have been scanned, the game ends a nd the player with the most points wins.
